Hello, I am a Software Engineer / Front End Developer and this is my website.

Originally from Wilmette, Illinois I moved to Las Vegas around the time I turned 21 to figure life out on my own. I lived there for 8 years while dealing poker at the WSOP/TI/Mirage/Venetian. A time came where I needed to make a career change. One that I would enjoy long term. So, I restarted school again for computer science with the intent of graduating from UNLV but along the way I came across an article discussing the rise in popularity of programming boot camps. Quickly I decided to move to the SF Bay Area in pursuit of my dream.

The journey hasn't been easy but has been immensely rewarding and ended up being one of the best decisions of my life.
