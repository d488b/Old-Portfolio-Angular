Hi, I am a Software Engineer / Front End Developer and this is my website.

Originally from Wilmette Illinois I moved to Las Vegas around the time I turned 21 to figure life out on my own. I lived there for 8 years and dealt poker for a living. A time came where I needed to make a career change. I began school again for computer science and the plan was to graduate from UNLV (University of Las Vegas Nevada) but along the way I came across an article talking about the rise in popularity of programming boot camps. I quickly decided to move to the SF Bay Area and pursue my passion. The journey hasn't been easy but has been rewarding and ended up being one of the best decisions of my life.
